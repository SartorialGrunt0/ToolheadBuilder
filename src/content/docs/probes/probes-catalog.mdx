## Touch Probes

Touch probes are the most traditional probes. They rely on a servo and pin to deploy and stow the probe pin.

- [BLTouch](https://www.antclabs.com/bltouch) - The OG probe, wide-compatibilibility, fragile pin, best suited for unenclosed printers.
- [3DTouch](https://trianglelab.net/products/3d-touch?VariantsId=10429) - Clone of BLTouch, available from many vendors, lower cost alternative.
- [CRTouch](https://www.creality3dofficial.com/products/creality-cr-touch?srsltid=AfmBOorpqiWGeJO0H0x2FTjj4KzZ_nlzrvoU4I_gdTsSAF7bQ9wUKxnQ) - Variant of BLTouch, features a metal probe pin.
- [MicroProbe](https://github.com/bigtreetech/MicroProbe) - Lightweight touchprobe.

## Dockable Probes

Dockable probes are the lightest as they are stored on the printer frame after probing.
They are are very simple in design, often based on a simple mouse microswitch.
Require a complex klipper macro to support the docking and stowing of the probe.

- [Klicky](https://github.com/jlas1/Klicky-Probe) - Dockable microswitch probe, features a mouse switch for probe, wide printer support, supports of fixed docking.
- [Klicky PCB](https://github.com/tanaes/whopping_Voron_mods/tree/main/pcb_klicky) - Dockable microswitch probe, variant of Klicky with a PCB main switch body.
- [Quickdraw](https://github.com/Annex-Engineering/Quickdraw_Probe) - Dockable microswitch probe.
- [Euclid](https://euclidprobe.github.io/) - Dockable microswitch probe, PCB main body.
- [ZeroClick](https://github.com/zruncho3d/ZeroClick) - Dockable microswitch probe for Voron V0.
- [Unklicky](https://github.com/majarspeed/Unklicky) - Dockable probe, high sensitivity conductive probe, many variations for different applications.
- [SideSwipe](https://github.com/chestwood96/SlideSwipe) - Dockable microswitch probe for Voron V0, features a servo dock.

## Nozzle Probes

Nozzle probes utilize the tip of the hotend nozzle to probe the bed.
This gives a very accurate probe measurement and do not require and X or Y offset.
This also requires that the tip of the nozzle be clean and heated to the ideal temperature before probing.
They are best paired with a nozzle wiper.


- [TAP](https://github.com/VoronDesign/Voron-Tap) - Probe designed to allow the X carriage to move in the Z direction for probing, uses a microswitch or optical sensor, often can be found in CNC aluminum or carbon fiber variants.
- [Boop](https://github.com/PrintersForAnts/Boop) - Similar to TAP, designed for Printers for Ants, features an optical sensor.
- [PZ](https://e3d-online.com/products/pz-probe?srsltid=AfmBOopCkk44Azg8ySMm6_5Gj9cQxeVgEav7dtdpjlHCE6du4UQm0JIE) - Nozzle probe featuring a piezoelectric sensor attached to the Revo Voron hotend.
- [ALPS](https://3dmellow.com/products/mellow-heatcore-4-uhf-alps-hotend-smart-leveling-nozzle-probe-for-voron-blv-vzbot-hevort-diy-3d-printer-high-flow-and-high-speed) - Nozzle probe featuring a pressure sensor attached to a Mellow HeatCore, FluxHeat, or V6 hotend.
