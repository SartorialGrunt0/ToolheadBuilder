Is there so many options you have developed choice paralysis? Here are our top picks.

---
## The Full Size: A4T

 [A4T](https://github.com/Armchair-Heavy-Industries/A4T) is the top pick for the Voron 2.4 and Trident. Its light, has strong cooling with dual 4010 blowers, accepts basically any combination of extruder, hotend, and probe, and is easy to adapt for multicolor. 
<img src="/A4T_render.webp" width="495" height="330" alt="A4T" loading="lazy" decoding="async" />

---

## The Micro-King: AntHead

[AntHead](https://github.com/PrintersForAnts/AntHead) is the go-to option for Printers for Ants. With its streamlined design, solid 4010 cooling, support for filament cutters, and support for a wide variety of extruders, probes, and hotends its no wonder its a popular choice. It even features a full build manual!
<img src="/Anthead_render.webp" width="495" height="330" alt="Anthead" loading="lazy" decoding="async" />

---

## Compatibly First: Dragon Burner

[Dragon Burner](https://github.com/chirpy2605/voron/tree/main/V0/Dragon_Burner) is a lightweight and versatile toolhead for Printers for Ants and full size printers. It supports a huge range of hotends and extruders, making it one of the most flexible options available. With its compact design and wide compatibility, it has earned its place as a top pick.
